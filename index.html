Un grupo unido jamas sera vencido!!

Sabor is Columbia University's first and hottest Latine dance troupe.
Established in 2003, we perform salsa, bachata, merengue and
many other styles of dance.

Our annual fall show celebrates Latine identity through dance, bringing stories to life on stage. Proceeds support Relevé, our spring outreach program for underrepresented high school students in NYC.
